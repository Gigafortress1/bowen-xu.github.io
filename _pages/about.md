My current advisor is [Prof. Ziyu Shao](https://faculty.sist.shanghaitech.edu.cn/faculty/shaozy/home.html).

My research interest includes Bandits & Reinforcement Learning, Deep Reinforcement Learning, Network Intelligence, Distributed AI System.
